Cannon AFB Naming Convention

The name is split up into 4 parts:

Identifier:
For Cannon this is CZQZ

Computer Type:
W, L, or T for workstation, laptop, or tablet

Squadron:
An abbreviated version of the squadron the computer belongs in

Serial:
The remaining spaces are filled with the beginning, middle, or end of the serial based on the manufacturer
